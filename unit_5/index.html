Our Story

My Grandfather went by the name Paty when he emigrated from Italy in 1945. He owned a deli in Brooklyn, and it's always been my dream to be a business owner. After living in New York City for 5 years, I learned a thing or two about the perfect bagel, and made it my mission to create my own. Lots of dough and family taste tests later, I'm so excited to bring my original recipe home to Newtown.
